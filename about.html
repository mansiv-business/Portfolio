How I Work: My Design & Development Approach

1. How do I design layouts and typography?

I primarily design using Figma, where I experiment with a variety of combinations involving text styles, font weights, colors, sizes, and font families. This allows me to create clean, modern layouts that perfectly fit each project's unique personality and goals.

2. How do I source and edit custom images?

To find the perfect visuals, I leverage AI tools alongside several trusted image sources. I then carefully edit these images to seamlessly blend with the overall design, ensuring every element supports the brand’s story and aesthetic.

3. What do I mainly focus on during development?

My expertise lies in web design. I specialize in creating engaging animations, responsive layouts, and custom typography that showcase your work at its best across all devices. While I don’t focus heavily on backend development, I am capable of handling essential tasks such as creating functional contact forms to meet your needs.

4. Why do I choose VS Code to develop websites?

VS Code is my go-to development environment because it allows me to manage complex coding tasks efficiently and keep your project organized. This ensures high-quality, maintainable code that helps your website perform smoothly and reliably.
