How I Work

1.Discover

I gather project info,goals and preferences to understand your need.

2.Design

I design layouts and content in Figma based on the collected info.

3.Develop

I build the site in VS Code and launch it on Github pages for preview.

4.Test & Confirm

I test the website content and designs using AI and get your final approval.

5.Deliver

I connect the domain and deliver the site using escrow.com with support.
